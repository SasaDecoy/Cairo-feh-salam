\section{Phase 1 \textbullet\ Pre-Cleaning Audit}
\label{sec:phase1-nulls}

\subsection{Why we audit before we clean}

A null audit is the cheapest form of due diligence. Before any imputation
runs, we count missing cells per column per dataset, classify the
missing-ness as either \emph{numeric-recoverable} or \emph{structural},
and decide an imputation policy. Without this audit we cannot defend
the choice of $k$ or the choice of features used to impute.
